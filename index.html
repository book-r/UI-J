About Profile

Welcome to Bookr!

BookR is a social platform and hub for all things text-book related. Whether you're a student or educator, there is a wealth of knowledge available to you. Looking for reviews of a specific text book? Scouring the web for the best text book deals for that college class? Want to review a text? Join our growing community and peruse our ever expanding database!

Our Vision

There was never really a social hub dedicated to text-books, so we created our platform to bring together like-minded individuals who are not aversed to voicing their real opinions and experiences with the books they have used in the past. We strive to provide a positive environment for our users to interact in while cutting out the clutter of other types of books.

A huge database of Books

Our database of books is always expanding due to community input. Looking for a specific book? It's likely already in our system!

Interactive community

Have a book you'd like to review? Add it to your account and start typing away! Community members may see your review and add their own!

Peer Reviewed Text Books

Whether you're an educator or a student, you can rest assured knowing that you'll find only the most honest reviews here. We are not sponsered by any companies-The books listed are reviewed by people just like you!
